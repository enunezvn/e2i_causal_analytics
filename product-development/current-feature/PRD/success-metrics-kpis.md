# Success Metrics & KPIs

## Product Success Metrics

### User Adoption

- **Target**: 80% of analytics team using platform weekly by Month 6
- **Metric**: Weekly Active Users (WAU) / Total Licensed Users
- **Current**: Baseline measurement in progress

### Query Volume & Engagement

- **Target**: 50 queries/user/week average
- **Metric**: Total queries / WAU
- **Leading Indicator**: Repeat usage (users returning within 7 days)

### Time to Insight

- **Target**: 90% reduction in time from question to validated answer
- **Baseline**: Median 3-5 days with manual analysis
- **Goal**: Median <5 minutes with E2I platform

### Decision Impact

- **Target**: 30% of commercial decisions cite E2I insights as primary input
- **Metric**: Survey of decision-makers + usage tracking
- **Validation**: A/B test outcomes align with E2I predictions >80% of time

## Technical Performance Metrics

### Query Performance

- **P50 Latency**: <1s (simple), <5s (complex)
- **P95 Latency**: <2s (simple), <10s (complex)
- **P99 Latency**: <5s (simple), <20s (complex)

### Causal Validation Quality

- **Refutation Pass Rate**: >85% of estimates pass all 5 tests
- **Confidence Interval Width**: <20% of effect size (95% CI)
- **Sensitivity**: E-value >2.0 for robustness to confounding

### Model Performance

- **Prediction Accuracy**: >80% for churn, conversion, adoption models
- **Drift Detection**: <24 hours from drift onset to alert
- **Twin Fidelity**: >80% accuracy on validation experiments

### System Reliability

- **Uptime**: 99.5% availability
- **Error Rate**: <0.5% of queries result in errors
- **Agent Coordination Success**: >94%

## Business Impact Metrics

### ROI Improvement

- **Target**: 15% improvement in marketing ROI within 12 months
- **Measurement**: Campaign ROI before/after E2I recommendations

### Resource Optimization

- **Target**: 10% reduction in wasted commercial spend
- **Measurement**: Budget reallocation based on causal insights

### Experiment Success Rate

- **Target**: 25% improvement in A/B test success rate via digital twin pre-screening
- **Measurement**: Compare success rate of twin-validated vs non-validated experiments

### Executive Confidence

- **Target**: 90% of executives report "high confidence" in causal insights
- **Measurement**: Quarterly stakeholder survey
