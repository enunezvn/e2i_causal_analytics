‚ö†Ô∏è The Fundamental Problem: Label Lag

ML predictions are made today, but ground truth (actual outcomes) isn't known until days, weeks, or months later. Without ground truth, we can only detect feature drift (input distribution changes) but not concept drift (relationship between inputs and outcomes changes).

‚ö° Edge Case Handling
Scenario Model Types Problem Handling
New HCP HCP Churn <3 prior scripts, insufficient baseline EXCLUDE Label INDETERMINATE, reduce confidence to 0.50
Data Source Lag All Prediction within lag period (7-14 days) DEFER Wait for lag_days + observation_window
Seasonal Effects HCP Churn Q4 holiday dips misclassified as churn FLAG Require 2 consecutive quarters
Multi-Trigger Script Conversion Multiple triggers in observation window FLAG Attribute to first, partial_attribution for rest
Therapy Switch Treatment Response Patient switched brand (not discontinuation) FLAG Label SWITCHED, separate category
Clinical Discontinuation Treatment Response MD-directed stop (disease resolved, AE) EXCLUDE Label APPROPRIATE_STOP
Market Disruption Market Share Competitor launch, recall, major event FLAG Label CONFOUNDED, lower weight
Trigger Not Delivered Script Conversion, NBA Trigger generated but not viewed EXCLUDE Exclude from conversion, flag for acceptance analysis
